01
The missing layer

Fastest route is not always the safest route.

Most navigation apps optimize for time and distance. Safety Route App adds a third dimension: road risk.

Our app already scores route options by lower risk. For this challenge, we strengthen that feature with ADB road-segment safety data.

02
App-first solution

ADB data lives inside the route decision.

Not a separate dashboard. Not only a report. The dataset powers safer route scoring, vehicle-specific risk, and route explanation inside Safety Route App.

04
Speed Safety Score

Every road segment gets a clear risk label.

The score combines speeding behavior, speed excess, traffic exposure, land use vulnerability, and road class.

Risk Score = Speeding Behavior + Speed Excess + Traffic Exposure + Vulnerability + Road Class
Low
0–25
Medium
26–50
High
51–75
Critical
76–100

05
Vehicle-aware safety

Same road. Different vehicle. Different risk.

Motorcycle

More sensitive to high-speed corridors, unsafe intersections, and urban conflict areas.

Car

Balanced scoring between travel time, safety risk, and traffic exposure.

Bicycle

More sensitive to high-speed corridors, unsafe crossings, urban conflict zones, and segments with heavy traffic exposure.

Vehicle selection makes the recommendation practical, not generic.
