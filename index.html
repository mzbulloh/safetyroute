The route gap

Current navigation often optimizes for fastest arrival. For people walking, cycling, or riding scooters, the safer route may be different.

Safety as a choice

Scooter mode

Higher penalty for fast corridors, heavy exposure, and risky road classes.

Bicycle mode

Prefers calmer links and avoids segments with high speed mismatch.

Pedestrian mode

Prioritizes lower-speed connectivity and safer urban crossing exposure.

Beyond just re-routing, we're explaining.

Every warning names the avoided risk: speeding, exposure, land use, road class, accident history, or flood-prone segment.
