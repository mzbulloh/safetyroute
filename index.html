Scooter mode

Pedestrian mode

Beyond just re-routing, we’re explaining.

Every warning names the avoided risk: speeding, exposure, road class, land use, under-reported vulnerable-user risk, or flood-prone segment.

Our AI reads every road

Using YOLO (You Only Look Once) computer vision, the system detects road actors, hazards, and context clues that help explain segment-level safety risk.

Computer vision detection

Finds road users, vehicles, and conflict situations in real scenes to support explainable segment-level risk.

AI safety-speed validation

Supports the safety speed reference layer by validating where environmental cues suggest higher caution is needed.

Interactive navigation

The interface adapts to the selected mode. Walk prioritizes the safest continuity, Cycle balances safety and travel time, and Scooter highlights when a slightly slower route is the safer recommendation.
